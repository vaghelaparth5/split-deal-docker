Welcome to SplitDeals

SplitDeals is your smarter way to split expenses with friends, family, and flatmates. Whether you're out dining, shopping, or traveling — we make it effortless to track, split, and settle shared bills.

We’re built for the modern social lifestyle. Automatic calculations, real-time updates, and transparent logs keep everyone on the same page. Powered by clean UI and a secure backend, SplitDeals makes money management easier than ever.
